Caretaker Model

Our local caretakers are trusted community members who help bridge the technology gap. They assist sellers in uploading products, managing inventory, and help buyers place orders. Caretakers also provide on-ground support for quality control and dispute resolution, ensuring smooth transactions even in areas with low digital literacy.

  • Help with product listing and inventory management
  • Assist buyers with order placement and tracking
  • Local quality control and verification
  • Dispute resolution and customer support

Secured Delivery System

Our delivery system ensures safe and efficient transportation of goods between villages. For local orders, caretakers coordinate hand-delivery or local logistics. For distant orders, we partner with reliable courier services using a hub-and-spoke model that prioritizes nearby items first and provides real-time tracking.

  • Distance-based sorting (nearby items shown first)
  • Local caretaker coordination for nearby deliveries
  • Professional courier partnerships for distant orders
  • Real-time tracking and SMS/app notifications
